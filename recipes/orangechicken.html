Orange Chicken

Description

Tender chicken thighs fried crisp and tossed in a magical perfect-copycat sauce!

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • soy sauce
  • orange juice
  • cornstrarch
  • garlic
  • rice vinegar
  • orange zest
  • siracha
  • brown sugar

Steps

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then let cool. Once cool, transfer to a zip-top bag with chicken and place in the refrigerator to marinate for at least 2 hours
  2. After the chicken is marinated, coat it in flour, salt, and pepper. Cook it in a skillet until it's brown on both sides.
  3. Add the sauce to an empty skillet and bring it to a boil. Add cornstarch and water (to thicken it), then the chicken pieces. Let it simmer until everything is combined.
